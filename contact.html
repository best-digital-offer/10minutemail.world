Frequently Asked Questions

Service Questions

How long do temporary emails last?

Temporary emails last for 10 minutes by default, but you can extend the time using the "+10 Minutes" button. The maximum duration is typically 60 minutes.

Is the service really free?

Yes, our temporary email service is completely free with no hidden costs, registration requirements, or premium tiers.

Can I recover expired emails?

No, for privacy and security reasons, expired emails are permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.

Technical Issues

Emails not arriving in my temporary inbox?

Some email providers may have delays. Wait a few minutes and refresh the page. If issues persist, try generating a new email address.

Website not loading properly?

Try clearing your browser cache, disabling ad blockers temporarily, or using a different browser. Our service works best with modern browsers.
