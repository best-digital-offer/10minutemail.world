Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

Our Commitment to Privacy

At 10MinuteMail.world, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our temporary email service.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our temporary email service:

  • Temporary Email Addresses: We generate temporary email addresses that are automatically deleted
  • Email Content: Emails received at temporary addresses are stored temporarily and automatically deleted
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve our service
  • Technical Information: IP addresses, browser type, and device information for security purposes

How We Use Information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Provide temporary email services
  • Deliver emails to temporary inboxes
  • Maintain service security and prevent abuse
  • Improve our service quality and user experience
  • Comply with legal requirements when necessary

Data Retention

We practice minimal data retention:

  • Temporary emails are automatically deleted after 10 minutes
  • Email content is permanently deleted when emails expire
  • No personal information is stored long-term
  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • When required by law or legal process
  • To protect our rights, property, or safety
  • With service providers who assist in operating our website (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • In case of business transfer or merger (users will be notified)

Third-Party Services

Our website may use third-party services:

  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics
  • Google AdSense: For displaying relevant advertisements
  • CDN Services: For faster content delivery

Security Measures

We implement various security measures to protect your information:

  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure servers with regular security updates
  • Automatic data deletion to minimize exposure
  • Regular security audits and monitoring
  • Access controls and authentication systems

Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for:

  • Essential website functionality
  • Remembering user preferences
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Advertising personalization (with consent)

Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your information:

  • Access: Request information about data we collect
  • Deletion: Request deletion of any stored data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Opt-out: Opt out of analytics and advertising cookies
  • Portability: Request data in a portable format

Cookie Management

You can manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block all cookies (may affect functionality)
  • Delete existing cookies
  • Set preferences for future cookies
  • Receive notifications when cookies are set

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information.

International Users

Our service is available globally. By using our service, you consent to the transfer of your information to our servers, which may be located in different countries. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.
